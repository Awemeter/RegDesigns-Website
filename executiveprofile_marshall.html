Marshall produces high quality physics based rendering textures for airport buildings and ground polys. Early in his career, Marshal mastered the art of projected meshes and custom ground polys.

Before joining RegDesigns, Marshall served as a freelance 3D asset modeller. He produced and textured renders of vehicles brands such as BMW and other car brands.

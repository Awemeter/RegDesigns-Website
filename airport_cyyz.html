Toronto Lester. B Pearson International Airport (CYYZ) is the largest airport within Canada, amounting to 50.5 million passengers visiting the airport in 2019. Furthermore, the airport serves as the second busiest gateway in the Americas and is the 30th busiest in the world by passenger traffic. As well, the airport is a hub for some of Canada's largest airlines, such as Air Canada, WestJet, and Sunwing Airlines, the aerodrome will provide a plethora of destinations to visit for your flight simulation excursions.
